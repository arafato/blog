More About Me.

Hi there! My name is Oliver Arafat and here’s my bio in a nutshell: Enterprise Evangelist at Amazon Web Services. Serious (ultra-)runner and cyclist, whenever I have the time. Husband to Cornelia, father of Lennie and Lilli.

I joined Amazon Web Services in April 2015 as an Enterprise Evangelist covering Germany. You can find some details of my activities on my LinkedIn Profile (along with a more complete version of my CV) so connect with me there if you’re interested in keeping up to date with what I’m up to.

Prior to joining AWS I spent over 3 years working for Microsoft as an enterprise consultant for cloud and web technologies where I helped customers in reaching their full potential with cloud-based solutions.

Even before that I was working for Siemens as a software architect and later on product manager for the first ever created embedded SOA middleware following the SCA standard.

If you’re interested in more regular updates and news you can find me on Twitter @OliverArafat.
